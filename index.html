Abstract
Gender classification through speech analysis aims at predicting the gender of the speaker from a given speech sample. In the field of signal analysis, this particular topic of interest has become a hot choice for research and development among scientists. The human voice consists of various parameters through which a analytical prediction can be made about the gender of the speaker.In this project, we have tried to do the same by mainly focusing on Autocorrelation factors and creating a threshold frequency for both male and female voices in a Matlab program.
1. Introduction
The voice information stored in the speech signal not only delivers a message but also hints at many properties such as age,gender,region of the speaker.In the field of technology gender detection has many applications . We know that Male has the fundermental frequencies & spectral formant frequencies lower than the female. Pitch is also a important feature which can be obtained to study the voice in both time domain and frequency domain. The speech waveform is directly analyzed and the methods like short time autocorrelation, modified autocorrelation through clipping technique, normalized cross correlation function, average magnitude difference function, square difference function etc. are used.

2. Proposed Approach
Using Pitch to classify Gender
Pitch period is defined as the time interval between two consecutive voiced excitation cycles.Its basically the fundermental frequency of the voiced samples.This process of fundermental frequency detection is also known as Pitch Detection.The reason behind using this method is that the average fundermental frequency for a adult man is 85-185 Hz and that for a woman is 165-200 Hz.But there are several challenges of using this method as it works well distinguishably only with non noisy speech samples.Also at some particular frequencies both male and female voices co-exist. However,Generally females posses higher fundermental frequencies than men.So this method of practice have been adopted in this experimentation.
Modiefied Autocorrelation Method
This method is based on the finding the highest value of Autocorrelation function.The autocorrelation of a periodic signal is periodic but since voice samples are not periodic,we have to divide the speech signal into small frames and then apply short time autocorrelation function for all frames and calculate pitch for every frame and then get the average pitch from all the frames.This can be easily processed in MATLAB through its built in command
r = xcorr(x,y)
It returns the cross-correlation of two discrete-time sequences, x and y.
